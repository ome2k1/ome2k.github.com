AI Experiments & Creations

  • Preventing Financial Fraud Machine Learning Techniques for Credit Card fraud detection

    Credit card fraud is a growing concern for financial institutions and consumers, causes significant losses to companies and consumers alike and traditional rule-based systems are often insufficient to detect new types of fraud. In this project, we will build a machine learning model using Decision Tree (DT), Na√Øve Bayes (NB) and Logistic Regression (LR) algorithms to detect fraudulent credit card transactions and to classify transactions as either fraudulent or legitimate. The model will be trained and tested on a highly imbalanced dataset containing 284,807 transactions, out of which 492 are fraudulent. We will preprocess the data by balancing the data, scaling, normalizing the features and then split it into training and testing sets. The performance of the proposed system will be evaluated using various metrics, including Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 score, and confusion matrix. The results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed system in detecting credit card fraud and its potential to be used in real-world applications to improve the security of financial transactions.

  • Arabic to English Language Tool

    The proposed project aims to develop an innovative Arabic to English Writing Improvement Tool, utilizing the Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technique to provide users with guidance on how to translate Arabic text into English effectively and fluently. The tool will leverage large language models (LLMs) trained on (How to write better English) books ,such as "The Elements of Style" by William Strunk Jr. and E.B. White, "On Writing Well" by William Zinsser, "The Oxford Guide to Writing" by Thomas S. Kane, to offer users two outputs: one providing a native-level translation of the Arabic text into English, and another providing a simplified and learner-friendly version suitable for English learners. This project aims to empower Arabic speakers to improve their English writing skills and facilitate English language learning by providing personalized and contextually relevant guidance
